MARK ZUKERBERG

THE CEO OF FACEBOOK

  • Mark Elliot Zuckerberg is an American programmer, Internet entrepreneur, executive, and philanthropist.
  • He is the chairman, chief executive officer, and co-founder of social networking website Facebook.
  • His net worth is estimated to be US$54.5 billion as of September 2016, ranking him as the 5th richest person in the world.
  • Since 2010, Time magazine has named Zuckerberg among the 100 wealthiest and most influential people in the world as a part of its Person of the Year distinction.
  • Zuckerberg launched Facebook from his Harvard dormitory room on February 4, 2004.
  • In a public Facebook post, Zuckerberg launched the Internet.org project in late August 2013.
  • Zuckerberg explained that the primary aim of the initiative is to provide Internet access to the 5 billion people who are not connected as of the launch date.
